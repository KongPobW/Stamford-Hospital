Mental Health

During COVID-19

As the coronavirus pandemic rapidly sweeps across the world, it is inducing a considerable degree of, fear, worry, and concern in the population at large and among certain groups, such as older care providers.

In particular, people with underlying health conditions as well. Despite facing great difficulties, many adolescents and young people have found different ways to face new challenges and cope with their emotions.

A recent poll conducted by UNICEF shows that the COVID-19 crisis is having a significant impact on the mental health of adolescents and young people in Latin America and the Caribbean.
