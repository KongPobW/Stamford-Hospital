Bleaching vs. Whitening: What’s the Difference?

According to FDA, “bleaching” is permitted for use when the teeth can be whitened beyond their natural color. This applies strictly to products containing bleach — hydrogen peroxide or carbamide peroxide. On the other hand, the term “whitening” refers to restoring a tooth’s surface color by removing dirt and debris.
