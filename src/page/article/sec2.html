Stay-At-Home

You should be self-quarantine if you may infect the virus. Also, if you have returned from a very high-risk area. In addition, if the coronavirus test shows that you are infected, you must self-isolate, if you are protected against coronavirus.

The government is campaigning for people to stay at their home for helping to reduce the spread of COVID-19. Dr. Patarawan Woratanarat, the head of Orthopaedic Department, Mahidol University, Faculty of Medicine Ramathibodi Hospital, agreed with lockdowns.
