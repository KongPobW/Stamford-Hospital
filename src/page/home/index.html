Executive (Female) Check-up Package is created for executive females aged from 46 years. As this stage is start of a golden age of health, organ function starts changing and can put you at risk for various diseases especially Coronary Artery disease.

Executive (Male) Check-up Package is created for executive males from 46 years. As this stage is start of a golden age of health, organ function starts changing and can put you at risk for various diseases especially Coronary Artery disease.

VIP (Female) Check-up package is created for females from 36-45 years who work under stress and have abnormal working hours that can affect their health. The scanning will concentrate on risk of heart disease and abdominal organs.

Executive (Male) Check-up package is created for males age between 36-45 years who work under stress and have abnormal working hours that can affect their health.

The Basic Plus Check-up Package is designed for working females and males, age 26-35 years and for healthy people who have annual check-ups to monitor their health.

Basic Check-up Package is created for females and males age 15-25 years. This package is a basic scanning of the organ systems designed to show any early signs of disease or ill health.
