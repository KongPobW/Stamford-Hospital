COVID-19

Information in Thailand

Coronavirus disease spreads primarily through contact with an infected person when they cough or sneeze. It also spreads when a person touches a surface or object that has the virus on it, then touches their eyes.

WHO Thailand is working closely with the Thai Ministry of Public Health and other national partners and in convergence with WHO's South-East Asia Regional Office and WHO Headquarters.

To rapidly expand scientific knowledge on this virus, to track its virulence and spread, and to provide advice to Thai authorities and citizens on measures protect health and prevent the spread of the outbreak.
